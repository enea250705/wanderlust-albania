About Wanderlust Albania

Your personal gateway to discovering Albania in the most authentic and eco-conscious way possible

Our Story

More Than a Travel Company

Wanderlust Albania is more than a travel company, it's your personal gateway to discovering Albania in the most authentic and eco-conscious way possible.

Our mission is simple: to meet every travel need for every type of visitor, while respecting and protecting the natural beauty of our country. Whether you're seeking relaxation, adventure, culture, or a mix of it all, we tailor your journey with care, detail, and full respect for the environment.

We proudly embrace a 100% eco-friendly vision, from green transport options to sustainable partnerships across the country. Every decision we make is guided by our commitment to responsible travel and meaningful experiences.

Our Values

What Makes Us Different

  • Passion for Albania

    We are driven by our love for Albania and our desire to share its beauty, culture, and hospitality with the world.

  • Eco-Friendly Approach

    Sustainability is at the core of everything we do, from the partners we choose to the experiences we design.

  • Personalized Service

    We believe that every traveler is unique, and we take the time to understand your preferences and create tailor-made experiences.

  • Authentic Experiences

    We connect you with the real Albania, beyond the tourist spots, for meaningful cultural exchanges and local connections.

  • Reliability & Trust

    Our commitment to excellence, transparency, and exceptional service has earned us the trust of travelers from around the world.

  • Community Support

    We actively contribute to local communities, supporting sustainable development and preserving cultural heritage.

We're By Your Side

From Arrival to Departure

  • Private Transfers

    Comfortable and convenient transportation from the moment you arrive in Albania, with eco-friendly options available.

  • Hand-picked Accommodations
