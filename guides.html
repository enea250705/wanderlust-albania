Sadjan Maraska

Sadjan Maraska is a licensed and experienced tour guide specializing in cultural and historical tours across Albania. With a deep passion for history and architecture, he provides visitors with an authentic and enriching experience while exploring ancient cities like Shkodra, Berat, and Tirana.

His tours offer insightful storytelling, local expertise, and a well-organized itinerary to ensure an unforgettable journey. Sadjan is fluent in multiple languages and has extensive knowledge of Albanian history, art, and traditions.
